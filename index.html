About Our School

Established in 1986, R.N. Tagore Higher Secondary School has been a beacon of quality education in the region. Our commitment to academic excellence and character development has shaped generations of successful individuals.

We believe in nurturing curious minds, fostering creativity, and instilling values that prepare students for the challenges of tomorrow. Our state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty create an environment conducive to learning and growth.

Our mission is to provide affordable, quality education that empowers students to become responsible global citizens while preserving their cultural roots.

Message from the Principal

"At R.N. Tagore, we strive to create an atmosphere where students can discover their potential and develop into well-rounded individuals. Education is not just about academics but about building character and life skills that will serve our students in all their future endeavors."

Proud Collaboration with Physics Wallah

We're excited to partner with Physics Wallah to bring quality online learning resources to our students. This collaboration enhances our STEM education with expert-led video lectures, practice materials, and competitive exam preparation.
